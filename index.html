Looking for Good Sushi, Better Service? Shark Sushi is the best Japanese eatery serving fresh and good portion of sushi, sashimi, specialty rolls, and sake bombs in Brea. We have made painstaking efforts to create the tidiest and cleanest dining place, and guarantee you with friendly and timely service. Whether it's your first visit to Shark Sushi or one of many, we invite you to settle in and savor all the pleasures we have to offer. And, we look forward to sharing our daily freshness with you and your family!
